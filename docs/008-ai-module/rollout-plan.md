# AI Module Phased User Rollout Plan

## Overview

This document outlines the phased rollout strategy for the AI Module to ensure smooth adoption and minimal disruption.

---

## Rollout Phases

### Phase 1: Internal Testing (Week 1-2)

**Objective**: Validate functionality with internal team

**Timeline**: March 15-28, 2026

**Participants**:
- IT Team (5 users)
- Operations Team (3 users)
- Inventory Management (2 users)

**Activities**:
1. Deploy to staging environment
2. Conduct user acceptance testing
3. Collect feedback and bug reports
4. Fix critical issues
5. Document known limitations

**Success Criteria**:
- 90% of test cases pass
- No critical bugs
- User satisfaction > 70%

---

### Phase 2: Pilot Users (Week 3-4)

**Objective**: Real-world usage with limited users

**Timeline**: March 29 - April 11, 2026

**Participants**:
- Warehouse Managers (5 users)
- Production Supervisors (3 users)
- Sales Team Lead (2 users)

**Activities**:
1. Deploy to production
2. Conduct training sessions
3. Assign dedicated support
4. Monitor usage patterns
5. Collect feedback

**Success Criteria**:
- 80% feature adoption
- Positive user feedback
- No major incidents

---

### Phase 3: Department Rollout (Week 5-6)

**Objective**: Full department adoption

**Timeline**: April 12-25, 2026

**Participants**:
- All Inventory Team
- All Production Team
- All Sales Team

**Activities**:
1. Company-wide announcement
2. Additional training sessions
3. FAQ distribution
4. Office hours for Q&A
5. Regular check-ins

**Success Criteria**:
- 70% monthly active users
- 50+ forecasts generated
- 20+ stockout predictions reviewed

---

### Phase 4: Full Launch (Week 7-8)

**Objective**: Organization-wide availability

**Timeline**: April 26 - May 9, 2026

**Participants**:
- All ERP users
- External stakeholders (read-only)

**Activities**:
1. Launch announcement
2. Marketing materials
3. Executive demo
4. Success stories
5. Continuous improvement

**Success Criteria**:
- 60% user adoption
- Positive ROI indicators
- Feature requests collection
